# 📊 Sales & Product Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 🌟 Project Overview
This project presents a dynamic and interactive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, and product profitability. By transforming raw data into visual insights, this dashboard helps businesses track their growth and identify key performance drivers.

---

## 🚀 Key Features & Insights

### 1. Financial Performance
* **Total Revenue:** Successfully tracked over **$19.80 Crore** in total sales.
* **Profit Analysis:** Visualized a total profit of **$8.57 Crore**, helping to identify high-margin areas.
* **Trend Analysis:** Identified 2015 as the peak revenue year ($54M) and analyzed the subsequent trends.

### 2. Employee & Regional Tracking
* **Employee Efficiency:** Created a **Treemap** to show revenue contribution by individual employees (e.g., Archer Lamble & Kayla Woodcock leading with $18M each).
* **Geographic Reach:** Integrated a Map visual to track sales distribution across North America and Europe.

### 3. Product & Customer Deep-Dive
* **Product Attributes:** Analyzed revenue based on **Size** and **Color** (e.g., Blue and Black being top performers).
* **Detailed Reporting:** Included a granular "Product Details" page for invoice-level tracking.
* **Customer Base:** Tracked a total of **402 unique customers** and **2.28 Lakh transactions**.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack Used
* **Power BI Desktop:** For Data Visualization and UI/UX design.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** For calculating complex measures like Total Profit, Revenue %, and YOY growth.
* **Power Query:** For Data Cleaning and ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes.
* **SQL:** Used for initial data understanding and structuring.

---

## 📂 Project Structure
* `Sales_Dashboard.pbix`: The main Power BI file containing the report.
* `Screenshots/`: Folder containing images of the Summary and Product Details pages.
* `Data/`: (Optional) The dataset used for this analysis.
